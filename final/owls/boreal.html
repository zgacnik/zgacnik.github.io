Boreal Owl - Aegolius funereus

There is a very good chance you have never heard of the boreal owl, because it is one of
the most rarely spotted owls in Montana. The boreal owl's small size and strict nocturnal
behavior makes it a difficult bird to find. These small owls can be found in boreal forests
across the western side of the state.
