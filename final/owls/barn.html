Barn Owl - Tyto alba

Barn owls are one of the most easily identifiable owls in the state of Montana. Their light
plumage and distinct facial pattern catch the eyes of many, but are still rarely spotted.
Look for these medium-sized owls swooping over fields and grasslands during dawn or dusk
in search of their next meal.
