Welcome!


The beautiful state of Montana is filled with a multitude of animal species.
But, none are quite as impressive as the owl! Here in Montana, we have 15
different owl species that visit throughout the year. Many species live here
year-round while others hang out for just a short time to breed. Taking a
nighttime stroll in the woods or even simply listening for them to call on
your front porch can leave you with an experience you'll remember forever!
