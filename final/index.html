Welcome!

The beautiful state of Montana is filled with a multitude of animals species.
But, none are quite as impressive as the owl! Here in Montana, we have 15 different
owl species that visit throughout the year. Many species live here year-round, while
others hang out for just a short time to breed. Taking a nighttime stroll in the woods
or even simply listening for them to call on your front porch can leave you
mesmerized. Have fun exploring this site to learn more about these spectacular,
nocturnal creatures!
