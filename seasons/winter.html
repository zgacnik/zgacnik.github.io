Winter Page

Winter time represents the closing of the year, bringing on cold temperatures
and lots of snow in most regions. Christmas and New Years are popular holidays
during the Winter and is often people's most favorite season throughout the year.
If you love chilly weather and hanging out in the snow, Winter is the perfect
season for you!
