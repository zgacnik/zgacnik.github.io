Autumn Page

Autumn represents the closing of the growing season, and all the leaves
on trees begin to fall. Temperatures start to cool down and even snow can
be seen during this time of year. Along with its comfortable temperatures,
Autumn brings on beautiful colors in nature and helps us transition into Winter.
